01 // Services & Location

What services do you offer, and where are you located?

expand_more

We are SV Creations Studio & Photography, located at Vaithiyasalai, Thambikottai, Pattukkottai, Tamil Nadu. We offer premium wedding photography, pre-wedding and post-wedding shoots, portrait sessions, birthday event coverage, cinematic videography, and drone photography. We provide the best services at the market rates starting from just ₹4,999.

02 // Lead Time

How far in advance should we book our wedding photography?

expand_more

To secure your date, we recommend booking 3 to 6 months in advance. Since we only capture a limited number of commissions per month to maintain our signature editorial quality, bookings are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis with a retainer fee.

03 // Creative Style

What is your photography style and approach?

expand_more

Our style is a blend of editorial fine art and candid, photojournalistic storytelling. We focus on capturing raw, authentic emotions, elegant details, and cinematic motion. We guide you naturally during portraits while remaining unobtrusive during key ceremonial moments.

04 // Delivery Timelines

How long does it take to deliver the photos and videos?

expand_more

For portrait sessions and pre-wedding shoots, we deliver high-resolution edited images via a private cloud gallery within 7 days. For full wedding collections and cinematic films, the delivery timeline is 3 to 4 weeks, ensuring meticulous color grading and cinematic editing.

05 // Travel & Coverage

Do you travel for destination shoots outside Pattukkottai?

expand_more

Yes, absolutely! While our main studio is located in Thambikottai, Pattukkottai, we serve the entire Thanjavur district, Tamil Nadu, and travel across India and internationally for destination weddings and pre-wedding campaigns.

06 // Custom commissions

Can we customize our photography packages?

expand_more

Yes, all of our packages can be fully customized to suit your unique vision, timeline, and budget. Whether you need additional event coverage, multiple photographers, high-end drone shots, or physical luxury albums, we will work with you to curate the perfect package.
